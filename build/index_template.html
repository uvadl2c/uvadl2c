About

Deep learning is taught in the MSc program in Artificial Intelligence of the University of Amsterdam. In this course we study the theory of deep learning, namely of modern, multi-layered neural networks trained on big data. The course is taught by Assistant Professors Efstratios Gavves and Wilker Aziz Fereira.
